Art Marketing & Professional Strategy

Developing your public presence, promoting your work, and building a site that serves your career.

The Ultimate Artist’s Website Checklist: https://www.artplacer.com/the-ultimate-artists-website-checklist-must-dos-must-haves-and-essentials/

A guide to structuring and improving your artist website. Covers must-have pages, homepage layout, design dos and don’ts, writing your bio, and tips for selling art online.

How To Make a Press Kit for a Visual Artist: https://gwennseemel.com/blog/2024/0108-press-kit/

A step-by-step guide with a full example. Covers what to include (bio, images, press quotes, contact info, etc.) and how to format it as a press-friendly PDF.

All Pages You Need For a Professional Artist’s Website: https://www.contemporaryartissue.com/all-pages-you-need-for-a-professional-artists-website/

Breaks down all essential (and optional) pages artists should include — from portfolio, CV, and contact to press, exhibitions, publications, and store — with guidance rooted in art world expectations.
