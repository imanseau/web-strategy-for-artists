Online Tools for Artists

Use these tools to create, enhance, or manage your portfolio site.

QR Code Monkey

Free tool for generating custom QR codes to link to your website, social profiles, contact pages, and more. Great for print-based sharing.

  • https://www.qrcodemonkey.com

Archive.org

A digital archive of websites and media. Use it to explore past versions of websites for inspiration or research.

  • https://archive.org

TinyJPG

Compresses images to make your website load faster without losing visible quality. Essential for optimizing gallery images.

  • https://tinyjpg.com

Google Analytics

Tracks visitor activity on your website. Helps you understand how users interact with your pages and where your traffic comes from.

  • https://analytics.google.com

Google Fonts

A free library of web-safe fonts you can use in your site design. Easily integrates with HTML and CSS.

  • https://fonts.google.com

Fontpair

Helps you find Google Font combinations that look good together, ideal for styling your siteâ€™s headings and body text.

  • https://www.fontpair.co

ColorHexa

A powerful color resource. Get hex codes, create color palettes, explore gradients, and understand color relationships.

  • https://www.colorhexa.com

Coolors

An intuitive color palette generator that helps artists and designers pick harmonious colors for their website branding.

  • https://coolors.co

Unsplash

Free, high-quality stock photos you can use on your website without worrying about copyright. Great for placeholders or design polish.

  • https://unsplash.com
