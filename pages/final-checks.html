Final Checks Before Launch

Make sure your site is ready for the public.

  • Your homepage is named index.html
  • Your site works on mobile and desktop
  • All links and buttons work
  • Contact form submits correctly
  • All images load and are compressed
  • Fonts and colors are consistent
  • You’ve tested with Lighthouse or PageSpeed Insights
  • You’ve written a short artist bio and uploaded your CV
  • You’ve included alt text on all important images
  • Social media/contact info is visible and working
