Topics in Studio Art:

AI for Artist Websites and Social Media Strategy

ARTS 4391.001

Welcome to AI for Artist Websites

This course is designed to empower you, the artist, to create a professional and compelling online presence without needing to write a single line of code from scratch.

Our Mission

We will demystify the technical side of web design by leveraging cutting-edge AI tools. You will learn to direct these tools to build a custom portfolio that reflects your unique artistic vision. We'll cover the essentials of modern design, content strategy, and social media marketing to ensure your website is not only beautiful but also effective.

How to Use This Site

This website is your central hub for the course. Use the main navigation to access:

  • Topics: Detailed guides and resources for each subject.
  • Exercises: Practical assignments to build your skills.
  • Examples: Inspiration and best practices.

Your Final Project

The goal of this course is for you to design and launch your own complete, professional artist portfolio. By the end, you will have a powerful tool to showcase your work, connect with your audience, and advance your creative career.
