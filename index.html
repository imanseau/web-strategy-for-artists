Topics in Studio Art:

AI for Artist Websites and Social Media Strategy

ARTS 4391.001

What This Course Covers

This course explores the application of Artificial Intelligence (AI) in creating and enhancing artist websites. We delve into AI-powered tools for web design, content generation, and developing effective social media strategies tailored for artists. Additionally, fundamental web design principles, including HTML, CSS, and user experience (UX), will be covered to ensure a comprehensive understanding.

How to Use This Site

This website serves as a central hub for all course materials. Use the navigation bar at the top to access topic pages, which contain detailed information, resources, and examples. The 'Exercises' page includes assignments to help you apply what you've learned.

What You Will Build

Throughout this course, you will work towards designing and building your own professional artist website or online portfolio. The skills gained will empower you to establish a strong online presence, showcase your work effectively, and reach a wider audience.
