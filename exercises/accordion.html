The Art of Collage

Explore three major movements in the evolution of collage-based art.

Pablo Picasso and Georges Braque introduced collage into fine art by integrating newspaper, wallpaper, and fabric into their Cubist compositions.

Artists like Hannah Höch and Max Ernst used collage to subvert norms, combining disjointed imagery to provoke, disturb, or reimagine reality.

Today, collage spans digital and analog forms, blending photography, glitch aesthetics, and found materials in experimental, narrative-driven formats.
