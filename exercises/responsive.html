Color Theory in Modern Art

Artists like Josef Albers explored the interaction of color through precise studies, showing how context alters perception. This informed both painting and design disciplines in the 20th century.

Texture in Sculpture

Tactile surfaces play a critical role in how viewers experience sculpture. From smooth bronze to jagged concrete, texture shapes both light and emotional response.

Space in Installation Art

Installation artists consider the viewer's movement through space as part of the artwork, blurring the line between object and environment.
