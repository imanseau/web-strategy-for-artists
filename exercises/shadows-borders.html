Monochrome Minimalism

Exploring the power of black and white to strip form to its essentials, this movement highlights contrast and space without distraction.

Color Field Painting

Large swaths of color evoke emotion and immersion. Artists like Rothko used massive canvases to create meditative visual environments.

Digital Collage

This hybrid practice layers scanned textures, glitch effects, and AI-generated forms to remix and reframe the contemporary image.
